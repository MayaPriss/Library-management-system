Purpose

Our aim is to provide free and open access to resources, services, and new technologies that promote life-long learning,
as well as to facilitate the pursuit of knowledge and the fulfilment of curiosity in our community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to support students and researchers through the transformative power of information, discovery and learning.
